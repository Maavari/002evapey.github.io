Documentation

Explaining what Cosmos actually is.

    General

    The foremost purpose and intention regarding Cosmos is to ensure the users in a Discord server get entertainment. That's why the main innovation of Cosmos is it's entertainment commands, and among it the Moderation capabilities.

    DM Messages

    Cosmos has a few commands that personally message the user, and personally messages the owner of a Discord server when Cosmos first gets added. We designed Cosmos this way so your server chats could stay nice and clean so that you could easily read what the bot had messaged you without your chat moving if you have an active server.

Features

More in depth explanations of Cosmos' features.

Advanced Dashboard

Manage your servers with Cosmos' advanced dashboard. You can configure almost every major aspect of Cosmos and tailor it specific to your server.

The Cosmos dashboard allows you to; configure greeting/farewell embeds in whichever channel you please, edit the prefix of Cosmos if you prefer prefixes over slash commands, configure the amount of warnings someone can recieve before a punishment (This is also the same with auto moderation), create a ticket system, and configure statistics.

If you prefer to not use a dashboard and just Cosmo's commands, everything is still available to configure within Cosmo itself.

Powerful Moderation & Auto moderation

Cosmos comes equipped with powerful moderation and (SOON) auto moderation modules. The moderation modules are more standard, however we are working hard to implement one of the most powerful automoderation modules that will be available.


Music

Cosmos boasts a dedicated private Lavalink host so that we can provide you with high quality, lossless music from Youtube, Soundcloud, Spotify and more without worrying about buffering. You can also apply bass boost to your songs for that extra spice.


Ticket system

Cosmos has a ticket system which by default is turned off. You can enable it either through the dashboard or via commands. You can configure multiple categories for tickets so that the tickets aren't all in one place and it is easier to manage, which also means you could have different staff members overlooking different categories of tickets.

Utility

The Utility category of Cosmos is home to the more miscellaneous commands. These are commands that we don't think would fit into any of the other categories, but we love too much to get rid of. this category includes the help command, among translation and weather commands.

Anime

These commands are more used within conversations as reactions to what people say etc.

Economy

Cosmos has an economy system just like many other Discord bots where you can beg for money, gamble, deposit money to keep it safe, pay other users etc.

Stats Tracking

Cosmos has stats tracking disabled by default. You can track your server's activity with Cosmos' levelling system and see who is the most active with leaderboards. You are able to configure the level up message to match your server's theme.

Fun Commands

Cosmos has plenty of fun commands to keep you preoccupied while waiting for friends, here you can learn facts about different animals, look at photos of animals, browse through memes, or play games directly through Cosmos

Image Manipulation

Cosmos has a plethora of image manipulation commands where you can apply filters like blur, pixelate, invert, distort etc. directly to people's avatars or links to images. You can also apply overlays to the provided images. Lastly you can generate memes for the provided image with up to 25 available generators.
